Hyderabadi Chicken Biriyani
⏰
Preparation Time: 1 Hr
🍔
Type: Non-Veg
Utensils: Cooking pot, Spatula, Mixing Bowl

Preparation

Follow these steps to make a delicious dish:

Marinate Chicken: In a bowl, mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, salt, and lemon juice. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours or preferably overnight for best results. Cook Rice: Rinse basmati rice until the water runs clear and soak for 30 minutes. In a large pot, bring water to a boil with cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, bay leaves, and salt. Add rice and cook until 70% done, then drain and set aside. Prepare Biryani Base: In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, heat ghee or oil. Add marinated chicken and cook on medium heat until the chicken is tender and cooked through. Layer the Biryani: Preheat your oven to 180°C (356°F) or prepare a heavy-bottomed pot with a tight-fitting lid. Layer half of the chicken mixture at the bottom of the pot. Add a layer of partially cooked rice over the chicken. Sprinkle fried onions, coriander, and mint leaves. Repeat layers, ending with a final layer of rice. Drizzle saffron milk over the top layer of rice. Seal the pot with aluminum foil and cover with a lid. Dum Cooking: Place the pot in the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes or cook on a low flame for 25-30 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and the flavors are well blended. Serve: Gently fluff the rice with a fork. Garnish with additional fried onions, coriander, and mint leaves. Serve hot with raita or a side of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 3 cup rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 5 onions
  • 2 tomato
