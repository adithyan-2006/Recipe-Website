Lets Beigin....


Ramen
⏰
Preparation Time: 40 mins
🍔
Type: Non-Veg
Utensils: Pan, Spatula, Mixing Bowl

Preparation

Follow these steps to make a delicious dish:

Prepare the Broth: In a large pot, combine chicken broth, water, kombu, shiitake mushrooms, ginger, and garlic. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes. Remove the kombu after 10 minutes. Stir in soy sauce, miso paste, and sesame oil. Adjust seasoning to taste. Cook the Noodles: Bring a separate pot of water to a boil. Add the ramen noodles and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. Prepare Toppings: While the broth simmers, prepare your toppings. Boil the eggs, peel, and halve them. Cook chicken and shred it. Blanch the spinach or bok choy in boiling water for 1-2 minutes, then drain. Assemble the Ramen: Divide the cooked noodles into serving bowls. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles. Arrange the boiled eggs, shredded chicken, spinach or bok choy, corn, scallions, and nori strips on top. Drizzle with chili oil for extra heat, if desired. Serve: Serve immediately while hot. Enjoy your delicious bowl of homemade ramen!

Ingredients

  • 4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 piece kombu
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 1 tomato
  • Sauces
  • fresh ramen noodles
