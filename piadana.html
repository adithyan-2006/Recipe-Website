Piadina
⏰
Preparation Time: 30 mins
🍔
Type: Veg
Utensils: Pan, Spatula, Mixing Bowl

Preparation

Follow these steps to make a delicious dish:

Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the olive oil and mix well. Gradually add the warm water and mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface until smooth and elastic. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 30 minutes. Shape the Piadina: Divide the dough into 6 equal portions. Roll each portion into a thin, round disc (about 20-25 cm in diameter). Cook the Piadina: Heat a non-stick or cast-iron skillet over medium heat. Place a rolled-out disc of dough onto the skillet. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown spots appear. Optionally, you can prick the dough with a fork to prevent puffing. Serve: Fill the warm piadina with your favorite ingredients, such as prosciutto, mozzarella, arugula, tomatoes, or any other fillings you like. Fold the piadina and enjoy it warm!

Ingredients

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 220 l Warm Water
