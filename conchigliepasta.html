Conchiglie Pasta
⏰
Preparation Time: 30 mins
🍔
Type: Veg
Utensils: Pan, Spatula, Mixing Bowl

Preparation

Follow these steps to make a delicious dish:

Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the conchiglie pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving about 1/2 cup of pasta water. Set aside. Prepare the Sauce: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion. Saut√© until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant. Add the diced tomatoes to the skillet and stir to combine. Let the mixture simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Creamy Addition: Stir in the heavy cream, grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, and dried basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the sauce simmer for an additional 5 minutes until it thickens slightly. Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked conchiglie pasta to the skillet with the sauce. Toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add a bit of the reserved pasta water until you reach the desired consistency. Serve: Transfer the pasta to serving bowls. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and additional grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Ingredients

  • 500g conchiglie pasta
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped origano
  • spices
