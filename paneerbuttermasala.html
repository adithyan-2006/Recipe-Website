Paneer Butter Masala
⏰
Preparation Time: 30 mins
🍔
Type: Veg
Utensils: Pan, Spatula, Mixing Bowl

Preparation

Follow these steps to make a delicious dish:

Prepare the Paneer: Heat oil and 1 tablespoon of butter in a pan over medium heat. Add the paneer cubes and sauté until they are golden brown. Remove and set aside. Prepare the Gravy: In the same pan, add remaining butter and cumin seeds. Let them splutter. Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears. Mix in the tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates from the mixture. Add tomato paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for another 5 minutes. Finish the Gravy: Stir in the fresh cream and garam masala powder. Mix well. Crush the kasuri methi between your palms and add it to the gravy. Let the gravy simmer for 5-10 minutes on low heat. Combine Paneer with Gravy: Add the sautéed paneer cubes to the gravy. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, ensuring the paneer absorbs the flavors of the gravy. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer cubed
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomato
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • Spices
